California based designer, Lauren Christine Henno, resides in charming Santa Barbara, California. Her design aesthetic is rooted in the beauty of California's Coastal Riviera. With a degree in Business Marketing from the University of San Diego and design studies at Florence University of the Arts, Lauren's experience and passion for both business and design have allowed her to establish her own boutique firm. She acquires design inspirtation from her travels abroad, but always looks to her origins and the beauty of Santa Barbara's Mediterranean coast for influence.
